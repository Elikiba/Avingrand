Luxury Experience

Since our inception in 2014, Avin Grand has been Benin City's premier destination for sophisticated hospitality. We pride ourselves on creating unforgettable experiences that blend traditional elegance with contemporary luxury.

Our commitment to excellence is evident in every detail, from our world-class amenities to our exceptional service standards. Whether you're seeking a luxurious retreat, fine dining experience, or an elegant venue for celebrations, we provide the perfect setting for life's most memorable moments.

Located in the heart of Benin City, our facilities represent the pinnacle of luxury hospitality in Nigeria, offering distinct luxury experiences under two roofs - each designed to exceed expectations and create lasting memories.
